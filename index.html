As Co-founder and Chief Product Officer

Think of Melodrive as as a virtual composer sitting inside a digital world. The composer listens to the events happening in an experience and creates music that matches them. On the fly.

For example, when a player gets a hit from an enemy the music can respond by being emotionally more angry or dramatic. This real-time generation process is possible thanks to advancements in AI techniques, patented by Melodrive, which enable flexible change to the music to suit any emotional setting.

As Music Algorithms Developer

Zya enables anyone with a PC, literally, to easily create high-quality music. People can start creating a song in any genre by using their own voice to hum a beat or baseline. They can also tap or touch to create the beat. After inputing their own music, users can mix in hooks, some of which are from hit songs. At launch Zya has Lady Gaga's "Poker Face" and "Just Dance," as well as pop hits like "Like a Virgin," "Born to Be Wild" and "Bye Bye Bye." Zya's technology then adjusts the various parts of the song to make them fit together.

  • Affiliation: Music Mastermind (now Zya)
  • Date: May 2010 - June 2012
  • Domain: R&D, Vocal Transcription, Automatic Key Detection, Automatic Accompaniment
